Privacy Policy

Introduction

TNC Group ("TNC Group," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personally identifiable information (PII) when you use our website, services, or products.

Collection of PII

We collect PII when you voluntarily provide it to us, such as when you create an account, sign up for a newsletter, or contact us for support. The PII we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other information that you provide to us.

We may also collect PII through your use of our website, services, or products. For example, we may collect information about your IP address, device type, browser type, and location. We may also collect information about your use of our website, services, or products, such as the pages you visit, the links you click, and the searches you perform.

Use of PII

We may use the PII we collect to provide you with our services, to communicate with you about your account, to respond to your requests, to improve our website or services, and to send you marketing messages. We may also use the PII to protect our rights or property, to comply with the law, or to cooperate with law enforcement authorities.

Disclosure of PII

We may disclose your PII to third-party service providers who help us provide our services. These service providers may include web hosting, email marketing, and payment processing providers. We will only disclose your PII to these service providers if we have good faith that it is necessary to provide you with our services, to protect our rights or property, or to comply with the law.

We may also disclose your PII to law enforcement authorities or other government officials if required by law or if we believe that it is necessary to protect our rights or property, to prevent harm to others, or to comply with the law.

Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to access, update, and correct your PII. You may also contact us and request to restrict the processing of your PII or object to the processing of your PII.

We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe. Please note that we may need to retain your PII for legal or business purposes.

Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your PII from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include using secure passwords, encrypting data, and limiting access to PII.

Acceptance of Policy

By visiting and browsing the TNC Group website, you, as a user, signify your acceptance of the terms and conditions set within this Policy. In the event of changes to the Policy, your continued usage will be construed as acceptance of those changes.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes to this Policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any queries regarding our Privacy Policy or the use of PII, please contact us via email at support@tncitgroup.com We will address your inquiry as soon as possible.

We honor all requests to remove information from our databases.

We offer tailored IT services to maximize growth, efficiency, data protection, and business continuity to guide you through the ever-evolving technology landscape. Collaborate with us for excellence.
